Case Study

FluxDesk Business Website

A business-facing website concept designed to receive project requests and present services in a clear, conversion-focused way.

Challenge

Small businesses need websites that do more than look good. The website must explain services, build trust and make it easy for visitors to request help.

Approach

  • Designed a professional landing page structure.
  • Added a request project form connected to EmailJS.
  • Created clear service cards for business users.
  • Included call-to-action buttons for quotes and meetings.
  • Used responsive HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Outcome

The result is a client-ready digital presence that can receive enquiries, present services and support a professional consulting brand.
