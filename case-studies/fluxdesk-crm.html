Case Study

FluxDesk CRM

Project: FluxDesk CRM

Challenge: Small businesses often lose leads and customer requests because they use scattered channels without one central dashboard.

Approach: Designed a CRM concept with lead capture, request tracking, admin dashboard and future integrations for database, authentication and reporting.

Outcome: The project creates a clear foundation for an MVP that can later grow into a paid beta product.

Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Supabase planning, EmailJS planning.
